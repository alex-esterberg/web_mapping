Map Description

This interactive map is designed to display categorized points of interest across Washington DC. Viewers can explore different types of locations such as theatres, bars, bicycle spots, and music venues by clicking on them

Overall the legend allows users to filter categories on and off. On the map itself viewers can click on a point/symbol and it reveals additional information through popups.
